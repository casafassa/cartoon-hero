About Judge Dredd

In the future, crime is so bad the law enforcement officers are also the judges, delivering swift sentences to the unending supply of violent criminals at the scene of their crimes. This is Judge Dredd. He is remorseless in his droconian use of force against the undesirable miscreants who menace the streets of Mega-City One.

Dredd's primary weapon, The Lawgiver, is replete with all manner of ordinance to take out scores of degenerate criminals unwilling to comply with his orders.

The qualities required to tame the teeming, warn-torn streets of the sprawling dystopia, Mega-City One:

  1. Inability to smile, constant frown
  2. Anticiapte violoence with overwhelming violence
  3. Lack empathy for pyschotic sociopaths and undesriables in general
